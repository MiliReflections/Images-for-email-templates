Let’s Begin Your Creative Journey

We’re absolutely thrilled to welcome you to Reflections.live! Your offer letter marks the beginning of a new chapter—and we couldn’t be more excited to have you join us as an intern.
At Reflections, we believe in giving space to grow, freedom to explore, and the tools to truly thrive. Your internship isn’t about strict rules or limited topics—it’s about discovering what makes you shine as a creator, a thinker, and a contributor.

What You Can Expect:

No Topic Restrictions

You're not limited to one kind of content here. Whether it’s short stories, opinion pieces, film reviews, cultural commentary, travelogues, poems, or even quirky experimental formats—you’re free to explore any subject that sparks your interest. This is your creative playground, so feel empowered to write what you truly care about.

Friendly & Skilled Mentors

Our mentors are here to support you, not supervise you like a classroom teacher. You’ll receive constructive feedback, real-time guidance, and industry insights in a way that encourages growth, not pressure. We’re genuinely excited to see you evolve, and we’ll walk alongside you, step by step.

Identify Your Niche

You may arrive unsure of what you like writing most—but that’s completely okay. This internship is a chance to try everything, from light-hearted content to deep-dive research. Over time, you’ll naturally discover your unique style, voice, and the themes you’re most drawn to. That’s your niche—and we’ll help you shape it.

Learn As You Go

There’s no such thing as a perfect first draft. Each task you do, each article you write, and each piece of feedback you receive is part of a larger learning curve. You’ll grow not just as a writer, but also as a thinker, researcher, and communicator. Mistakes? They're just milestones in your learning path.

Enjoy Your Work

Above all, we want you to have fun. Writing should never feel like a burden here. Choose topics you enjoy, work at your own rhythm, and let your passion lead the way. When you love what you’re writing, your readers will feel that energy too.
You’ll be hearing from us shortly with onboarding steps, your workspace access, and a quick guide to get started. If you ever have questions or ideas—or just want to talk about your favorite writer—we’re always a message away!

Welcome to the world of reflections, where your voice finds its home.
